## üåü What is MathMint Quiz?

MathMint Quiz is a fun, educational game where you:

**Best part:** You can play without connecting a wallet first!

## üöÄ Getting Started

### Option 1: Play Without Wallet (Recommended for First Time)

1. **Open the demo** in your browser
2. **Click "Start Quiz!"** - No wallet needed!
3. **Answer 5 questions** - You have 15 seconds each
4. **See your results** - Points earned, correct answers

That's it! You can play as many times as you want without a wallet.

### Option 2: Connect Wallet to Save Progress

To save your scores on the blockchain and earn NFTs:

1. **Install MetaMask** (if you haven't already)
   - Visit: https://metamask.io
   - Install browser extension
   - Create or import wallet

2. **Get Test Bitcoin (tBTC)**
   - Visit: https://faucet.mezo.org
   - Enter your wallet address
   - Request testnet tBTC (free!)
   - Wait for confirmation (~1 minute)

3. **Connect Your Wallet**
   - Click "Connect MetaMask" on the demo page
   - Approve connection in MetaMask popup
   - MetaMask will prompt you to add Mezo testnet
   - Click "Add Network" and "Switch Network"

4. **Play Quiz with Wallet Connected**
   - Complete the quiz (5 questions)
   - Click "Submit Score to Blockchain"
   - Approve transaction in MetaMask
   - Wait for confirmation
   - Your score is now saved on-chain!

## ‚ö° MUSD Boost Feature (Advanced)

Want to earn MORE points? Use the MUSD boost!

### What is MUSD Boost?

- **Regular score:** 10 points per correct answer
- **With MUSD boost:** 15 points per correct answer (1.5x multiplier!)

### How to Use MUSD Boost

1. **Get MUSD tokens**
   - You need at least 1 MUSD in your wallet
   - Ask in Mezo Discord for testnet MUSD
   - Or acquire on Mezo testnet DEX

2. **Enable the Boost**
   - Connect your wallet
   - If you have 1+ MUSD, you'll see a gold boost section
   - Check the "Enable Boost" toggle before starting quiz
   - Play the quiz normally

3. **Submit Boosted Score**
   - Complete the quiz
   - Click "Submit Score to Blockchain"
   - Your points will be automatically multiplied by 1.5x
   - Example: 30 base points ‚Üí 45 boosted points!

**Note:** MUSD boost requires wallet connection and is checked on-chain for security.

## üé® Minting Your NFT Badge

When you reach 50+ total points, you can mint an achievement NFT!

### Steps to Mint

1. **Check Your Total Score**
   - Click "Check My Total Score"
   - Needs to be 50 or more

2. **Check Eligibility**
   - Click "Check NFT Eligibility"
   - Should say "‚úÖ Eligible!"

3. **Mint Your Badge**
   - Click "Mint NFT Badge"
   - Approve transaction in MetaMask
   - Wait for confirmation (~30 seconds)
   - Success! üéâ

4. **View Your NFT**
   - Open MetaMask
   - Go to "NFTs" tab
   - Your MathMint Badge will appear there!

**Important Notes:**

- Requires 50+ total points (not just one quiz)
- Small gas fee in tBTC required
- Badge is an ERC-721 NFT stored on Mezo L2

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Do I need cryptocurrency to play?**  
A: No! You can play the quiz unlimited times without any crypto. You only need tBTC if you want to submit scores to blockchain or mint NFTs.

**Q: Is this real money?**  
A: No, this is on Mezo TESTNET using test tokens (tBTC). Everything is free and for learning purposes.

**Q: Can I play on mobile?**  
A: Yes! Works on mobile browsers with MetaMask mobile app installed.

**Q: How long does each quiz take?**  
A: About 1-2 minutes (5 questions √ó 15 seconds + reading time)

### Wallet & Blockchain Questions

**Q: Why do I need to add Mezo testnet?**  
A: Smart contracts are deployed on Mezo Bitcoin Layer 2 testnet, not Ethereum mainnet.

**Q: What if I run out of tBTC?**  
A: Visit https://faucet.mezo.org to get more (free, unlimited for testnet).

**Q: How much does it cost to submit scores?**  
A: Very small gas fee (~0.0001 tBTC or less). Basically free on testnet.

**Q: Can I use a different wallet?**  
A: Currently supports MetaMask. Unisat/Leather support may be added in future.

### MUSD Boost Questions

**Q: Where do I get MUSD?**  
A: Ask in Mezo Discord community, or acquire on Mezo testnet DEXs.

**Q: Does MUSD get spent when I use boost?**  
A: No! You just need to HOLD 1+ MUSD in wallet. It's never spent.

**Q: Can I use boost without MUSD?**  
A: No, the contract checks your MUSD balance on-chain before applying multiplier.

### NFT Questions

**Q: Can I mint multiple NFTs?**  
A: No, only one badge per wallet address.

**Q: Can I sell my NFT?**  
A: It's a testnet NFT, so no real value. But yes, technically transferable as ERC-721 standard.

**Q: What does the NFT look like?**  
A: Metadata is stored on IPFS. It's an achievement badge showing you completed the quiz challenge!

### Leaderboard Questions

**Q: How do I get on the leaderboard?**  
A: Submit high scores. Top 5 total scores across all players are shown.

**Q: Does the leaderboard reset?**  
A: No, scores are permanent on the blockchain.

**Q: Can I see my rank if I'm not top 5?**  
A: Currently only top 5 shown. Check your total score with "Check My Total Score" button.

## üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

### Quiz Not Loading

- Hard refresh browser (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R)
- Check internet connection
- Try different browser
- Clear browser cache

### MetaMask Not Connecting

- Make sure MetaMask extension is installed
- Unlock MetaMask
- Check if you're on a supported browser
- Try refreshing the page

### Network Switch Failing

- Manually add Mezo testnet to MetaMask:
  - Network Name: `Mezo Testnet`
  - RPC URL: `https://rpc.test.mezo.org`
  - Chain ID: `31612` or `0x7b7b`
  - Currency Symbol: `tBTC`
  - Block Explorer: `https://explorer.test.mezo.org`

### Transaction Failing

- Check you have enough tBTC for gas
- Verify you're on Mezo testnet (not other network)
- Try increasing gas limit in MetaMask (Advanced settings)
- Wait and try again (network might be busy)

### MUSD Boost Not Showing

- Verify you have 1+ MUSD in connected wallet
- Make sure wallet is connected
- Check browser console for errors (F12 ‚Üí Console tab)
- Try refreshing page

### NFT Not Appearing in MetaMask

- Wait a few minutes (can take time to sync)
- Manually import NFT:
  - Contract Address: `0x03672f6b20622176554a4C0ba7B037d9dCE531f0`
  - Token ID: (check with "Check Mint Status" button)
- Check on Mezo explorer if mint transaction succeeded
